Submitting Your Project to Lumet Film Festival

A note regarding the 2021 Lumet Film Festival & the COVID-19 Pandemic: We recognize that the global impact of COVID-19 has greatly affected storytellers and their projects, from production halts to disrupted festivals premiere plans. As we constructed the policies and procedures for submitting projects for 2021 Festival consideration, we worked to account for and address those disruptions. To that end, we have extended each of our deadlines and will also consider projects that were previously invited to other events that were subsequently canceled or postponed.
